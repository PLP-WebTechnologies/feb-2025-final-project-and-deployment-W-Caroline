About Our School

Founded in 1985, J&R High School has been a pillar of academic excellence and community development for over three decades. Our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that prepares students for success in college, career, and life.

With a student-teacher ratio of 12:1, we offer personalized attention to each student. Our curriculum combines rigorous academics with innovative programs in STEM, arts, and athletics, fostering well-rounded individuals ready to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

Our History

What began as a small community school with just 200 students has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving over 1,200 students annually. Our campus has expanded to include state-of-the-art facilities while maintaining the close-knit community feel that has always been our hallmark.

Accreditation & Awards
